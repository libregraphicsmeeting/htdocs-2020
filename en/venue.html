Venue

The Libre Graphics Meeting (LGM) 2020 will take place in 3 venues.

  • ISTIC, at Rennes 1 University, in the computer science and electronic section ;
  • Jardin Moderne, a place receiving music artists and offering them some studio or music station on GNU/Linux. They also have a good place for the meeting;
  • Activdesign, working place of the LGM 2020 organisation team, an art school using only free software.

First place

We have an auditorium for conferences and 3 rooms for working groups and workshops.

Participate !

The Libre Graphics Meeting (LGM) is an annual international convention for the discussion of free and open source graphics software. You will assist to conferences and workshops about the free software graphics projects and learn advanced usage. And you will meet face to face all the marvelous people creating awesome software you already use or will use soon.

Accomodation

Hostel

There are severals hostels in Rennes. The historical center of the city is pretty and doable walking. The center of the city is bigger and accessible by bus and metro. More large circle of Rennes probably needs a car or a bicycle. Be careful there are not a lot of rentabike places.

Appart by local people

If you look for a little flat, some local people propose to rent their flat. Most of them are in the center of the city. Look for online flat renting services to find them.

Appart by AppartCity

If you look for a little flat, there are also dedicated solutions like appartcity.

Travel

Rennes has an international airport. Most of the local fly are going to or coming from Paris. You can also take the train. There is a high speed line from Paris (TGV), which takes 1h30. There are several train stations in Paris, keep in mind that the TGV going to Rennes starts from the train station Gare Montparnasse. You can also choose to come by car or by bus.

Contact us if you need some information.
