The Libre Graphics Meeting (LGM) is an annual international convention for the discussion of free and open source graphics software. The first Libre Graphics Meeting was held in March 2006.

LGM 2020 will take place in several venues with large, medium, and small spaces. We welcome lectures, workshops and other interactive formats.
